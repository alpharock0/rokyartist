About

Alfarouq Alderwish b. Libya — based Dubai, UAE
“Libyan-born artist based in Dubai, working under the name Roky. My practice centres on stencil and spray paint — high-contrast, symbol-driven work exploring institutional accountability, humanitarian memory, and identity. I use the visual language of international law and historical iconography against itself. I am also a first-year Architecture student at Heriot-Watt University Dubai.”
  • Medium Hand-cut stencil, spray paint, digital (Krita)
  • Practice Institutional accountability · Humanitarian memory · Identity · Political silence
  • Series Institutional Silence · R.O.K.Y. · The Leader
  • Education Architecture, Heriot-Watt University Dubai
